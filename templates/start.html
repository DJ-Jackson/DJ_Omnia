Who Is OMNIA DAS?

OMNIA

In English, OMNIA translates roughly to all. This app was started as a way to keep track of all of the shows watched by people everywhere.

DJ

DJ is the team's main back-end engineer, and has taken the idea started by Solomon and made it a reality.

Alton

Alton is the team's main front-end engineer. He has designed the entire website and has contributed heavily to the release of the website.

Solomon

Solomon is team lead. He came up with the idea for the app and designed the original layout of the website.
